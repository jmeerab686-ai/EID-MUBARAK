Eid Sunnahs & Duas

  • Take a bath (ghusl), wear your best clothes, apply perfume (for men).
  • Eat something sweet (dates) before Eid prayer.
  • Give Zakat al-Fitr before the prayer.
  • Take different routes to and from the prayer place.
  • تقبل الله منا ومنكم — “May Allah accept from us and from you.”
